How to upload videos on youtube

Many people's are multi talented but they can't show their talent to world. But you can show your talent to world using youtube and other social media platforms, If you know teaching then upload videos related to teaching on youtube. If you know coding upload your videos related to coding on youtube. Many people's use youtube for entertainment purpose. Some people's use for educational purpose and some for listening musics, etc. To upload videos on youtube follow steps given below.
1. Open Youtube app.
2. Click on Plus icon at mid-bottom.
3. click on upload a video.
4. choose video.
5. click on next in top-right corner.
6. again click on next in top-right corner.
7. give title and description(description is optional) to your video.
8. click on upload video.

After this your short video will be uploaded on youtube. Also watch below video.
