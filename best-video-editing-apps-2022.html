Kinemaster

Kinemaster is the one of the most powerfull video editor on android. It has more features than any other video editing apps on android. kinemaster provide feature like triming video, adding filters and trasition, chroma key, etc. One of the famous feature is chroma key. Chroma key feature is used in many hollywood movies. Chroma key feature is used to change backround. Orientation of Kinemaster is landscape it does't portait orientation. But minus point of kinemaster is watermark. For removing watermark you need use paid version of kinemaster. Some people download apk from some third party websites and apps. Vita

Vita also provides feature like kinemaster like chroma key, triming video, adding filters, adding trasition, etc. Vita video editor also have almost same featurs like kinemaster and is fully free. You can remove watermark for free. In Vita app setting you will get option of turning on and off watermark. Its your choice that if you want watermark then keep it turned on. And if you don't want watermark then turn it off.
